Our Journey So Far

Accenture‚Äôs Successful Execution of USD 2.7 Billion Strategic Deal with FedEx

Accenture has successfully executed a landmark USD 2.7 billion deal with FedEx, demonstrating its expertise in large-scale workforce transformation and seamless service transition. This collaboration marks a pivotal step for FedEx as it advances its digital and operational capabilities, with Accenture playing a critical role in driving this transformation through efficient planning, rapid execution, and seamless integration.

Phase 0: Project Prometheus Execution Timeline

  • Initial Discussions and Headcount Movement: Accenture initiated discussions and headcount planning with FedEx in early March 2024, setting the foundation for the successful ramp-up and transition phases.
  • First Ramp-Up Successfully Fulfilled: By April 1, 2024, FedEx requested an internal ramp-up of 400+ resources, which Accenture fulfilled within three weeks, meeting the target by April 22, 2024.
  • Re-Badging of Eviden Atos Resources: A critical milestone was the re-badging of Eviden Atos resources working on the FedEx account. Despite complexities, Accenture navigated the process effectively, securing the agreement after six weeks. The agreement, signed in the first week of June 2024, included Accenture‚Äôs payment of a fee to access the Atos resources, ensuring continuity and expertise retention.
  • Service Transition and Cut-Over Agreement: The service transition cut-over between FedEx and Eviden Atos was scheduled for September 1, 2024. However, FedEx requested that all Eviden Atos resources be on-boarded by August 1, 2024, requiring Accenture to compress its 12-week plan into just 7 weeks.

Biggest Deal in ATCI History with Unprecedented Complexity

This deal stands as the largest in the history of Accenture Technology Centers in India (ATCI), involving the onboarding of 1200+ resources across multiple locations within an aggressive timeline of 7 weeks. The scale and complexity of the engagement demanded meticulous planning and flawless execution.

  • Complexity in Staffing Models:
    • Re-badging of Eviden Atos Resources: Retaining critical knowledge and domain expertise by successfully transitioning 800+ resources.
    • Lateral Hiring: Quickly onboarding experienced professionals to fill specialized roles and ensure continuity in FedEx‚Äôs operations.
    • Fresher Induction: Integrating fresh talent to enhance capacity and meet future demands.
  • Seamless Integration Across Locations: Resources were onboarded across 6+ locations, requiring extensive coordination across recruitment, HR, and mobilization teams to ensure a smooth transition and operational readiness.

Accenture‚Äôs Accelerated Execution and Operational Excellence

  • Rapid Onboarding with Zero Disruption: Accenture successfully onboarded 1200 resources by August 1, 2024, ensuring seamless integration without any disruption to business operations. This accelerated execution was made possible through the coordinated efforts of over 100 Full-Time Equivalents (FTEs) across multiple teams, including HR, PTT, Recruitment, Mobilization, and Business.

Inclusion and Diversity at Accenture

Our Commitment

At Accenture, our commitment to inclusion and diversity begins with our leadership and is sustained by the efforts of all our people. We strive to advance a culture of equality where everyone feels they belong and can thrive.

How We Foster a Culture of Equality

We embrace a wide view of diversity and believe no one should be discriminated against based on age, disability, race and ethnicity, gender identity, gender expression, sexual orientation, or religion. To support our people, we offer:

  • Specialized training and mentoring programs
  • Networking support and flexible work arrangements
  • Mental health resources and equal benefits to same-sex couples where permitted
  • Accessible technologies and resources for persons with disabilities

Gender Equality

We believe the future workforce is an equal one. We're committed to achieving gender balance by 2025. Currently, 48% of our global workforce are women, with 45% representation on our board of directors and 26% on our global management committee.

Cross-Cultural Diversity

Embracing cultural diversity enhances our ability to innovate and support our global clients. Understanding and leveraging the diversity of multiple cultures helps us be One Accenture.

Neurodiversity

Neurodiversity describes natural variations in the human brain and relates to differences in how individuals think, learn, process, and behave. We're on a journey to create a neuroinclusive working environment that supports neurodivergent individuals to thrive.

Disability Inclusion

We're creating an accessible, inclusive environment where all our people can belong and thrive. Persons with disabilities at Accenture have access to the latest technology, resources, and training for a barrier-free workplace.
